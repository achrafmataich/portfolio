From {{'2023-07-10' | date}} to {{'2023-10-10' | date}} Centre Hospitalo Universitaire Hassan II - Fès

Detecting ECG Anomalies with AI

Driven by a passion for applying AI to solve real-world problems, I embarked on an R&D internship focused on developing an AI model capable of detecting anomalies in electrocardiograms (ECG). ECGs, which record the electrical activity of the heart, are crucial diagnostic tools in cardiology. However, manually analyzing ECGs for abnormalities can be time-consuming and prone to human error. My goal was to create an AI model that could automate this process, improving the accuracy and efficiency of ECG analysis.

To achieve this goal, I employed TensorFlow, a powerful open-source machine learning library, and utilized Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, a type of recurrent neural network (RNN) particularly well-suited for processing sequential data like ECGs. I trained the LSTM model on a large dataset of ECG recordings, carefully labeled to indicate the presence or absence of anomalies. The trained model demonstrated remarkable accuracy in identifying anomalies, outperforming traditional manual analysis methods.

This internship not only provided me with valuable hands-on experience in developing AI models for healthcare applications but also fueled my passion for exploring the intersection of AI and medicine. I am confident that AI has the potential to revolutionize healthcare, and I am committed to contributing to this field by developing innovative AI solutions that improve patient care.

From {{'2022-06-15' | date}} to {{'2022-08-15' | date}} Institu supérieur de l'ingénierie et des affaires - Fès

Optimizing Football Lineups with KNN and Flask

During this internship, I tackled the challenge of developing a football lineup recommendation system to assist coaches in making informed decisions about player positioning. Leveraging the power of the K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm, a versatile machine learning technique, I crafted a system that analyzes player attributes and historical performance data to determine their most suitable positions on the field. This system can help coaches identify the optimal lineup for each match, maximizing the team's potential and achieving strategic advantages.

In contrast to relying on external libraries, I meticulously coded the KNN algorithm from scratch in native Python 3.9, gaining a deeper understanding of its inner workings and enhancing my programming skills. This hands-on approach enabled me to tailor the algorithm specifically to the requirements of the football recommendation system.

To further enhance the system's accessibility and user-friendliness, I utilized the Flask web framework to create a RESTful API. This API allows coaches to seamlessly interact with the system, inputting player information and receiving tailored lineup recommendations. The API also facilitates the integration of the system into existing coaching software and tools.

This internship provided me with an invaluable opportunity to apply my machine learning and programming expertise to a real-world sports application. The experience reinforced my belief in AI's transformative potential in enhancing sports performance and empowering coaches to make data-driven decisions. I am committed to exploring further applications of AI in the sports domain to optimize strategies and elevate athletic performance.
